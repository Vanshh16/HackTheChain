Introduction to Turing HacX

Welcome to HackTheChain, a flagship hackathon where innovation meets collaboration, organized by the Indian Institute of Information Technology Kota (IIIT Kota) in association with Codebase. The hackathon will be held from 24th to 26th March, 2023. The event will be held virtually on Devfolio with a prize pool of â‚¹1,50,000.

HackTheChain is a platform for students from all over the country to come together, showcase their skills, and learn from industry experts in a competitive and collaborative environment. Our aim is to foster innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills while promoting a culture of collaboration and community-building.

Our hackathon is unique in that it offers participants two different tracks to choose from: the Blockchain Track and the Open-to-All Track. In the Blockchain Track, participants will have the opportunity to build decentralized applications and explore the full potential of blockchain technology. In the Open-to-All Track, participants are free to explore any domain of their choosing and can build web or mobile applications, data analysis tools, or anything else they can think of. HackTheChain is more than just a hackathon; it's an opportunity to network with like-minded individuals, gain valuable experience, and win exciting prizes. We provide participants with access to cutting-edge tools and technology, mentorship from industry experts, and a supportive community to help bring their ideas to life.

Join us for an unforgettable weekend of coding, learning, and fun. Register now and become a part of the HackTheChain community!

Frequently Asked

Questions

We got answers to the questions that you might want to ask about TuringHacX hackathon

Can I participate remotely?
HackTheChain is a virtual event, so you can participate from anywhere in the world!
I am a beginner, can I participate?
Yes, absolutely! HackTheChain is open to participants of all skill levels, including beginners. It's a great opportunity to learn and gain experience, meet new people, and work on a fun project. We encourage everyone to participate, regardless of their experience level.
How much does it cost to participate?
HackTheChain is completely free to participate in. All you need is a computer and an internet connection!
What is the maximum team size?
The maximum team size is 4 members. You can form a team with your friends or you can join a team with other participants.
How to register for HackTheChain?
You can register for HackTheChain by registering on Devfolio. You can find the link to register on the home page.
Can I participate without a team?
Yes, you can participate without a team. You can join a team with other participants or you can form a team with your friends.
Is HackTheChain only for blockchain developers?
No, HackTheChain is not only for blockchain developers. Although it has a track related to blockchain, it is an open hackathon and welcomes participants from all backgrounds and skill levels.
