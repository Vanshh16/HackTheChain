About Hackathon

Welcome to HackTheChain, a flagship hackathon where innovation meets collaboration, organized by the Indian Institute of Information Technology Kota (IIIT Kota) in association with Codebase and Google Developers Student Club (GDSC) IIIT Kota. The hackathon will be held from 24th to 26th March, 2023. The event will be held virtually on Devfolio with a prize pool of â‚¹1,50,000. HackTheChain is a platform for students from all over the country to come together, showcase their skills, and learn from industry experts in a competitive and collaborative environment. Our aim is to foster innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills while promoting a culture of collaboration and community-building. Our hackathon is unique in that it offers participants two different tracks to choose from: the Blockchain Track and the Open-to-All Track. In the Blockchain Track, participants will have the opportunity to build decentralized applications and explore the full potential of blockchain technology. In the Open-to-All Track, participants are free to explore any domain of their choosing and can build web or mobile applications, data analysis tools, or anything else they can think of. HackTheChain is more than just a hackathon; it's an opportunity to network with like-minded individuals, gain valuable experience, and win exciting prizes. We provide participants with access to cutting-edge tools and technology, mentorship from industry experts, and a supportive community to help bring their ideas to life. Join us for an unforgettable weekend of coding, learning, and fun. Register now and become a part of the HackTheChain community!
